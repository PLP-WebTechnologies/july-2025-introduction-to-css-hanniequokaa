History of Women in Coding

Introduction Pioneers Impact

Introduction

Women have played a vital role in the history of computer programming. From the earliest algorithms to modern-day innovations, their contributions have shaped technology as we know it.

Pioneers

  • Ada Lovelace – Known as the first computer programmer, she wrote the first algorithm intended for a machine.
  • Grace Hopper – Developed the first compiler and popularized the term “debugging.”
  • Katherine Johnson – Mathematician whose calculations were critical to NASA’s space missions.

Impact

These pioneers paved the way for diversity in technology and inspired future generations of women to pursue careers in programming and computer science.
